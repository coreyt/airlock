## enhanced_passthrough.py:157 investigation (Site 12) — FLAGGED, not forced

`_inject_or_append_system_prompt` runs inside `EnhancedPassthroughProvider._resolve_request`,
a `litellm.CustomLLM` provider-execution method. The only metadata dict in scope is built
fresh at :171 as `metadata = dict(resolved_params.get("metadata") or {})`, stamped with
`airlock_skip_fathom_logger=True` and `no_log=True`, and handed to the INNER nested
`litellm.completion(**resolved_params)` call. That inner call is explicitly suppressed
from Airlock logging — so a ledger record written there never reaches the outer request's
`data["metadata"]` that OBS-served/headers/logger consume. The outer request ledger is not
reachable here without threading a new parameter through the litellm-defined
`CustomLLM.completion(self, model, messages, ...)` contract (a DO-NOT-absorb cross-file
change). This injection is also a guardrail-ordering-independent fallback that duplicates
Site 11 (`enhanced_interceptor`), which records the inject on the normal path.

Decision: do NOT force / do NOT thread a new param. Flag Site 12 as unreachable in
output.json.blockers_encountered. Covered sites (1-11, 13-17 + drop_params) form a
correct, mergeable partial ledger.
