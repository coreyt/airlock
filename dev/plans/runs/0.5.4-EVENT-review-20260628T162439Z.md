## Verdict: CONCERN

### 1. [low] Recorder dispatch iterates a live mutable sink list
Refs: request_event.py:263, request_event.py:277

`dispatch()` iterates `self._sinks` directly. If a sink (or another thread) calls
`register()` during dispatch, the in-flight dispatch can observe newly-appended
sinks — not a stable snapshot. Low risk since registration is startup-only, but a
`tuple(self._sinks)` snapshot (optionally lock-guarded) better satisfies
deterministic dispatch under concurrent/reentrant use.
